Prepositions: The Magic Words That Show How Things Connect!

Welcome to the wonderful world of prepositions! These special words help us understand where things are, when things happen, and how things relate to each other. Join us on an adventure to learn about words like 'in', 'on', 'under', 'beside', and many more!

What Are Prepositions?

Prepositions are words that show the relationship between a noun or pronoun and other words in a sentence. They can tell us about:

  • Position or location (in, on, under, above)
  • Direction (to, toward, through)
  • Time (before, after, during)
  • Purpose or reason (for, because of)
  • Manner (with, without, by)

The cat is on the table.

The books are in the backpack.

We'll meet at the park after school.

Common Types of Prepositions

📍

Place

in, on, under, above, below, beside, between, among, at, by

The puppy is under the bed.

⏰

Time

at, on, in, during, before, after, since, until, for

Let's meet at 3 o'clock.

🚶

Movement

to, toward, through, across, into, onto, out of, from

We walked through the forest.

🔍

Manner

with, without, by, like

She drew the picture with crayons.

🎯

Purpose

for, to, in order to

I brought a gift for you.

💬

Agent

by, with

The cake was made by my mom.

Preposition Fun Facts!

Did You Know?

There are about 150 prepositions in English, but we only use about 70 regularly!

📚

Word Magic

The word "preposition" comes from Latin, meaning "to place before"!

🌍

Around the World

Some languages, like Japanese, put their prepositions AFTER nouns!
